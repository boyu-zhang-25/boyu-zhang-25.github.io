My notes on Ph.D. applications:
---
Hi, I get many questions regarding Ph.D. applications in HCI, Social Computing, and other related fields. While I am certainly not the top student to answer them, I will do my best to remain open, inclusive, and helpful, especially for students from underrepresented groups and individuals with special needs. 

Below are some materials I found very useful during my application season (Fall 2021). I will (try to) keep this page updated with the latest information. I hope you can find the resource you need from this page. If you have any additional questions, please feel free to reach out!

- [My SoP](http://boyu-zhang-25.github.io/files/SOP.pdf): This is the most frequently asked one. I am liberal about making my application essays open-access, but please excuse my poor writing.

- [A Twitter Thread](https://twitter.com/fadeladib/status/1353020072885940224?s=20) by Prof. Fadel Adib.

- Blogs by Nelson Liu: [NLP Ph.D. Programs](https://blog.nelsonliu.me/2019/10/24/student-perspectives-on-applying-to-nlp-phd-programs/) and [SoP](https://blog.nelsonliu.me/2020/11/11/phd-personal-statement/).

- [Ph.D. Applications FAQ](https://docs.google.com/document/d/1lT-bsIP0GKfh8l5sQnM2hCzzR9prt-QLx16rimUOdIM/edit) by Prof. Noah Smith.

- [Inside Ph.D. admissions](https://nschneid.medium.com/inside-ph-d-admissions-what-readers-look-for-in-a-statement-of-purpose-3db4e6081f80) by Prof. Nathan Schneider.

- [A Five-Minute Guide](https://web.archive.org/web/20200727191900/https://pg.ucsd.edu/PhD-application-tips.htm) and [The Ph.D. Grind](https://web.archive.org/web/20190930200154/http://pgbovine.net/PhD-memoir/pguo-PhD-grind.pdf) by Prof. Philip J. Guo.

- [Some Suggestions on writing your statement of purpose](https://www.cc.gatech.edu/fce/people/jmankoff/gradschool/sops.html) by Prof. Jennifer Mankoff.

- [Syllabus for Eric's PhD students](https://docs.google.com/document/d/11D3kHElzS2HQxTwPqcaTnU5HCJ8WGE5brTXI4KLf4dM/edit) by Prof. Eric Gilbert.
